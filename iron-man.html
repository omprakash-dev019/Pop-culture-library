IRON MAN

TONY STARK · STARK INDUSTRIES
ORIGIN STORY

Genius, Billionaire, Inventor

Anthony Edward Stark, captured by terrorists, built a suit of armor to escape. From that cave, with a box of scraps, Iron Man was born. He later refined the technology, revealing his identity to the world as the invincible Iron Man — a hero not by accident, but by choice.

“I am Iron Man.”

POWERS & TECHNOLOGY

Genius intellect

Inventor of groundbreaking tech, AI, and the Iron Man suits. Expert engineer, physicist, and strategist.

Arc reactor

Miniaturized clean energy powers the suits and keeps shrapnel from his heart. Can emit directed energy pulses.

Flight systems

Repulsors and thrusters allow hypersonic flight, orbital insertion, and extreme maneuverability.

Weaponry

Repulsor beams, uni-beam, missiles, and lasers – all integrated with advanced targeting.
