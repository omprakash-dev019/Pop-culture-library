THOR

GOD OF THUNDER · ODINSON
ORIGIN · ODIN'S HEIR

Born of Asgard

Thor Odinson, son of Odin and Jord (the earth goddess), was destined to be king of Asgard. His reckless pride led to banishment on Midgard, where he learned humility and became a true protector. Worthy of Mjolnir, he embraced his role as the God of Thunder, defending the Nine Realms.

“Whosoever holds this hammer, if he be worthy, shall possess the power of Thor.”

POWERS & ABILITIES

Thorforce

Vast superhuman strength, control over lightning and storms. Can summon thunderbolts and create hurricanes.

Immortality

As an Asgardian, Thor ages slowly and is immune to disease. His godly constitution grants near-invulnerability.

Flight & Speed

Using Mjolnir or Stormbreaker, he can fly at incredible speeds across realms.

Warrior's Skill

Millennia of combat training make him one of the most formidable hand-to-hand fighters.

WEAPONS & ARTIFACTS

Mjolnir

The enchanted hammer forged in the heart of a dying star. Grants control of weather and returns when thrown.

Stormbreaker

Ax-hammer crafted to counter the Infinity Gauntlet. Can summon the Bifrost and is even more powerful.

Odin's Armor

Belt of strength (Megingjörð) and the armored suit passed down from his father.

Járngreipr

The iron gloves necessary to wield Mjolnir’s power safely.
